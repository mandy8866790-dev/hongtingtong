Author's Note

作者介绍 About the Creator

您好，我是本装置的创作者。我致力于探索物理交互与数字媒介之间的边界。在这个充斥着快速滑动和碎片化信息的时代，我希望通过实体硬件与视觉影像的结合，创造一种具有“仪式感”的私人叙事空间。

Hello, I am the creator of this installation. I am dedicated to exploring the boundaries between physical interaction and digital media. In an era flooded with rapid scrolling and fragmented information, I hope to create a private narrative space with a sense of "ritual" by combining physical hardware and visual imagery.

档案碎片 Archive Fragments

这五个短片并非随机组合，而是代表了五种潜藏在我们记忆深处的情感切片。从校园深海里的压抑与救赎（《电话卡》），到赛博未来中对旧时光的依恋（《游戏卡》）。每一张实体卡片，都像是通往某段封存记忆的物理钥匙，等待着被唤醒。

These five short films are not a random combination; they represent five slices of emotion hidden deep within our memories. From the suppression and redemption in the deep sea of a high school campus ("Phone Card"), to the attachment to old times in a cyberpunk future ("Game Card"). Each physical card acts as a physical key to a sealed memory, waiting to be awakened.

听筒的隐喻 The Metaphor

“红听筒”是本作的核心隐喻。屏幕上的画面永远是静音循环的，就像生活中那些冷冰冰的旁观视角。只有当您亲自拿起听筒，建立起物理连接的那一刻，声音才会流入耳中。我想表达的，正是这种对“真实连接”的渴望——在这个喧嚣的世界里，有些故事只能被靠近、被倾听。

The "Red Receiver" is the core metaphor of this work. The images on the screen are always looping in silence, much like the cold, bystander perspectives in life. Only when you personally pick up the receiver and establish a physical connection will the sound flow into your ears. What I want to express is this very longing for "authentic connection"—in this noisy world, some stories can only be approached and listened to.

寄语 Message to You

当您握住听筒的那一刻，希望您能感受到物理触感带来的心跳。请允许自己在这短暂的几分钟里，隔绝周遭的杂音，沉浸在这些影像与原声中。愿您在这里，能找到属于您自己的共鸣与平静。

The moment you hold the receiver, I hope you can feel the heartbeat brought by the physical touch. Please allow yourself, in these brief few minutes, to isolate the surrounding noise and immerse yourself in these images and original sounds. May you find your own resonance and peace here.
